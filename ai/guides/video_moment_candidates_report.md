2. Click to the candidate timestamp; listen for ~60 seconds.
3. If the moment matches the SIO's key_claim, edit the SIO:
   - `timestamp_start_seconds: <integer>`

   - `media_verification_notes: "Confirmed by listening on YYYY-MM-DD; the moment matches the SIO's key_claim about <topic>."`
4. Re-run `npm run validate-media` to confirm no rule violations.

**For a `manual_review_required` candidate:**

1. Use the listen-for cues to skim the video by ear.
2. When you find the moment, follow the steps above.
3. If the moment can't be pinpointed (which is fine for a `talking_point`):
   - leave timestamp_start_seconds: null
   - the UI will show "Watch the source" and play from the start.
