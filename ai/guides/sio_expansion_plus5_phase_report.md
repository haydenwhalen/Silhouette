## 5. Verification method
Every SIO: official/credible-channel confirmation (yt-dlp `--print` channel_id + duration), speaker confirmation, caption pull, verbatim phrase search (`/tmp/vtt_merge.py`), context-aware timestamp, honest media notes. **Manual (human) captions:** Wambach, Westover, Minchin (highest reliability). **ASR auto-captions (normalized, artifacts noted):** the rest — e.g., Brady "I thank God"→"I think, God"; Mitchell "bold"→"bald" / "fat ass"→"fat arse"; Garmus name ASR error in interviewer line. Every clip hand-bounded to exclude adjacent clinical/crisis content (Ohashi excludes later body-shame; Green excludes the doc's mental-health frame; Harjo hard-bounded away from a DV content-warning; Westover bounded to the decision beat).
