I've had long periods of not writing much, and during those periods of not writing much, life is a bit boring, and it can be depressing. And I've realized that one of the necessary areas of discipline in my life has been to discipline myself not to fall into despair when I'm not writing, and crucially not to think, that's it, I'm completely finished.
