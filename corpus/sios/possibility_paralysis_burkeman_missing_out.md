The solution to the fear of missing out is to see that you're definitely going to be missing out on almost everything, whether you like it or not. The truth for finite humans is that every decision to do anything with an hour of your time is the decision to let go of an infinity of alternative possibilities for that time. But every decision we make in life, we're waving goodbye to whole alternative lives that we will never get to experience.
