The gap between who you are and who you say you want to be doesn't get crossed by a big move. It gets crossed by what you choose to do in the next hour. You already know what the person you want to become would be doing right now. You're not unclear. You're not under-resourced. You're deferring — to a future version of yourself who will somehow feel more ready than you do at this moment. That version doesn't exist. The next hour is the lever. Make one more call. Do one more rep. Have the conversation you've been avoiding. Your future is built or betrayed in the small windows you already control today.
