If you believe that there is one thing that you were ordained to do… well then you're always going to be dissatisfied, as you bounce from one thing to another, arms folded, saying nope, that's not it, that's not it. What if instead we could simply say, I am choosing to be passionate about what I do, and my purpose is whatever I am doing.
