About your own future, there's good uncertainty and there's bad uncertainty. People who are so certain of the future, who know exactly where they're going — I often say, how boring, how uninteresting. Life with a bit of uncertainty is fun, it's excitement, it's like a spice inside of it, it's a bit of mystery. If I planned everything about my life, if I tried to make everything certain, I wouldn't be able to live, I wouldn't enjoy myself.
