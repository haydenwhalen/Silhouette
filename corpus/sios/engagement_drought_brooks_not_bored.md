What happens typically is, in one's mid-40s, after working really hard and just seeing things get easier and being congratulated because you do a good job — you're like, 'I'm kind of burning out, I'm kind of feeling bored by this.' That's a signal that actually things have gotten harder. So your dentist at 45 starts taking Fridays off. 'I used to love being a dentist, but, you know, everybody gets bored.' They're not bored. They're actually on the wrong side of their fluid intelligence curve.
