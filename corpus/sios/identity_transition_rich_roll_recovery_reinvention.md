Getting sober removed the substance but not the question underneath it. For years I was compelled to repair all of that and get back to the person I was before — with blinders on. I never stopped to ask what I actually wanted, only what I was supposed to want. Everything in my life had always been programmed. I had never been without a track. Leaving that track felt terrifying — but staying on it was slowly killing something that mattered more.
