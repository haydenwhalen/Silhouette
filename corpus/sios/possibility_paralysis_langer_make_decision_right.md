The point isn't that options are identical — it's that the obsessive search for the guaranteed-correct choice is mostly wasted, because the rightness is built after you commit, not discovered before. For a possibility-paralysis user stuck trying to be certain they won't pick wrong, it relocates the work from choosing perfectly to engaging fully with whatever they choose."
