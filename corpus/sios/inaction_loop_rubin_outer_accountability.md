Obligers respond readily to outer expectations, but they have a lot of trouble meeting their own internal expectations. Like a friend of mine who said, "When I was on the track team in high school I never missed a track practice, but I can't make myself go running on the weekends." That's an obliger. Obligers are motivated by external accountability.
