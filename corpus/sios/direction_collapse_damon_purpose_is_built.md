Purpose is the latest developing capacity that I've ever studied, and so don't get too worried if it takes you a while to get there. Some people really don't find their purposes until they're well out of college, or way past their first job and they're in their late 20s, 30s and so on. So don't be impatient — as long as you're moving forward, learning, trying out things, it will come.
