I remember feeling really down about it, and a bit like, oh God, I'm never going to get anywhere. And the thing I remember saying to myself was: whatever is weird about you and weird about your way of seeing things and doing things is probably the thing you should be doing — but you have to do it more, because if you do it more, then it becomes something.
