My first observation: you will never make it. That's probably not what you guys want to hear this morning, but I invite you to see it as fabulous, outstanding news, for I don't believe there's actually an 'it.' I don't think it exists. One of the greatest gifts that you can give yourself right now is to decide, without apology, to commit to the journey and not the destination.
