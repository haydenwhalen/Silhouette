Motivation is fickle. Motivation comes and goes. I mean, you think about motivation, something as simple as being hungry can sap your motivation. Right? That's ridiculous. You can't rely on that. You have, oh my blood sugar went down, I'm not motivated anymore. Oh, I didn't get enough caffeine, I'm not motivated. No, wrong answer. Motivation is unreliable. When you're counting on motivation to get your goals accomplished, you're likely going to fall short. So don't count on motivation. Count on discipline.
