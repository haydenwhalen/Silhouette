The framework I found, which made the decision incredibly easy, was what I called a regret minimization framework. So I wanted to project myself forward to age 80 and say, okay, now I'm looking back on my life. I want to have minimized the number of regrets I have. And I knew that when I was 80, I was not going to regret having tried this. I knew the one thing I might regret is not ever having tried, and that that would haunt me every day. And so when I thought about it that way, it was an incredibly easy decision.
