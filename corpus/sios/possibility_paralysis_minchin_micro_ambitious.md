I never really had one of these dreams, and so I advocate passionate dedication to the pursuit of short-term goals. Be micro-ambitious. Put your head down and work with pride on whatever is in front of you. You never know where you might end up. Just be aware: the next worthy pursuit will probably appear in your periphery, which is why you should be careful of long-term dreams.
