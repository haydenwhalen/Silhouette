You have to rebuild yourself somehow, mentally, psychologically, afterward, because you question everything at that point. Now everything you have done seems like a failure, like the last three years that I prepared for this have went to waste. I'm a much better athlete than I was four years ago, but I was a champion four years ago, and now I'm nothing.
