We will — or again, I will — resist doing things for fear of feeling very, very low levels of difficulty or distress or embarrassment or awkwardness. It's not because I've got a terrible problem with my emotions and when I take that action I feel completely destroyed. It's just: tiny little bits of discomfort are enough to be like, ah, I'll do something else.
