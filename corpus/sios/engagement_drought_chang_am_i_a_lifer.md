When you're eight years into somewhere, you ask yourself: am I a lifer, and is this what I want to do for the rest of my career? Or, like — what is happening here, what do I actually want? And if there is something where, right before you close your eyes to go to sleep and right when you open them when you wake up in the morning, if you keep finding yourself thinking about something — or thinking to yourself, 'I should be happier, why am I not happier? I should be more grateful for what I already have.'
