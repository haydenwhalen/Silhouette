Well, pick something that you don't want to do. These are what I call in a very non-scientific way micro sucks. These things suck, but they suck a little bit, and they're safe. You have to pick things that are safe for you, but they suck enough that they require some effort, they require getting over some friction, engaging in something that you don't reflexively want to do.
