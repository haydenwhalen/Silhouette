Choose the big thing, and then be very selective and careful about the other things that you do. There's a lot of stuff you do have to say, not now, not yet. It doesn't mean I don't like it, it doesn't mean I don't care, but if I say yes to this, I'm going to be saying no to something more important, more breakthrough.
