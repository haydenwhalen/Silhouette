The thing that organized your whole life does not have to be something you loved. Agassi hated tennis — but it was still the container everything else was organized around. When it was gone, the relief and the emptiness arrived simultaneously. Knowing that you needed to be free of it does not mean you know who you are without it. Structure is not the same as meaning, but removing structure exposes you to questions that the structure was answering whether you asked them or not.
