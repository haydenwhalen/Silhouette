The guilt doesn't help in any way — it doesn't fuel me to keep going, it stops me from doing something I know I actually love doing. Give yourself a bit of grace. It's not going back to the beginning, it's just stepping back and stepping forward again. You don't have to feel like you've failed or ruined everything by taking a break from something.
