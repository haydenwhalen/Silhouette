And now I'm wandering far away from everything that I've known, far away from anything that has a name or a sign or a road signal. And I'm in the middle of the forest or the meadow and I'm terrified, I'm washed with confusion and fear that comes over me because I'm truly lost. But I'm also perhaps the most free I've ever been, and everything I feel, every step I take is something new to me. It is a discovery.
