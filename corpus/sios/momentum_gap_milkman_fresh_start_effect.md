At the start of a new year, we feel like we have a clean slate. Last year's failings feel far behind us, and we feel new, improved, and more capable than before. It turns out that it's not just at the start of a new year when we have those fresh start feelings. We feel those fresh start feelings at the start of lots of new cycles, like the beginning of a new week or a new month.
