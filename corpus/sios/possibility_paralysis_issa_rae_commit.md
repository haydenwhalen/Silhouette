To my credit, I took all the courses to be able to declare, but when it came time to answer the simple question of why I wanted to major in political science, I could only think, 'Because my dad wanted me to.' And so I didn't. I wanted to be a writer, I wanted to pursue film, TV, and at the time, theater. And I wanted to tell our stories, but I was also too scared to pursue it as anything but a hobby, until Stanford, and the community I built here, changed that for me.
